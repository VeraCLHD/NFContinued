When you look at a bottle of oil in the grocery store, there’s a “best-before” date, advising consumers how long they have to consume it before it starts going rancid, and builds up oxidation products that can be particularly harmful to human health.Here are the best-before dates for eight culinary oils: almond oil, avocado oil, hazelnut, macadamia, grapeseed, rice bran, toasted sesame, and walnut oil.
