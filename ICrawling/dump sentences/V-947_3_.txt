An overdose of a homeopathic remedy might seem a contradiction in terms, but just because they’re no better than a sugar pill doesn’t mean you can stuff babies full of sugar pills.Many homeopathic pills are made of xylitol, which can have a laxative effect, and cause diarrhea— which, if untreated in an infant, can turn into severe life-threatening metabolic acidosis, and land her in the ICU.
