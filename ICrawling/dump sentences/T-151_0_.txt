Artificial sweeteners are man-made substitutes for table sugar in food and beverages.Popular artificial sweeteners include: acesulfame K (Sweet One®), aspartame (Equal, NutraSweet®), cyclamate (Sugar Twin®), erythritol ( Zsweet®), saccharin (Sweet’N Low®), stevia (Truvía®, Pure Via®),  sucralose (Splenda®), xylitol (XyloSweet®) and sorbitol.Acesulfame K has been associated with various cancers in studies.
