There are studies showing lower concentrations in the breast milk or fat tissue of vegetarians, but what about vegans?They found that vegans were significantly less polluted than omnivores regarding a whole list of carcinogenic industrial toxins and pesticides—even after controlling for age and weight, which makes the difference in contamination even more dramatic, because, of course, the vegans had less body fat.What surprised the researchers was that the vegans had as much as they did.
