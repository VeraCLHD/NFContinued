Two-thirds of the recurrent canker sore sufferers had high levels of anti-cow’s milk protein antibodies in their bloodstream.The thought is that the cow’s milk proteins themselves penetrate deep into the lining of the mouth, inducing a major immune disorder—where our body attacks the foreign cow proteins, and in doing so, ulcerates our mucosal lining.To test this, a few of the patients stopped eating all dairy; a cow’s milk protein-free diet.
