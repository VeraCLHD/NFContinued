People don’t typically eat whole coconuts, though, so let’s break it down:Coconut oil, widely touted on the internet for its health properties.Coconut milk, which is now being used to make a variety of nondairy products.And dried coconut flakes.Now, none of these should be confused with coconut water, which is the fluid inside coconuts— which, interestingly, is so close in composition to human plasma that in an emergency, you can use coconut water intravenously.
