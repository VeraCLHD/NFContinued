Gooseberries are arguably the most important medicinal plant in Ayurvedic medicine, and also used in traditional Chinese and Thai medicine.They have been shown in preclinical studies to potentially possess, anti-fever, anti-pain, anti-cough, anti-artery-clogging, anti-stress, heart-protective, stomach-protective, anti-anemia, anti-cholesterol, wound-healing, and anti-diarrheal properties.
