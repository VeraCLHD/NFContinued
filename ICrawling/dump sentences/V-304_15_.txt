The worst five things about our diet is that we don’t eat enough fruit, we don’t eat enough nuts and seeds, we eat too much salt, too much bacon, hot dogs, lunch meat, etc., and not enough vegetables.That’s based on data like this, on diet quality and chronic disease mortality risk, which found that those scoring higher using a variety of different systems that all agreed on more whole plant foods, reduced the risk of dying from heart disease, cancer, and all causes of death combined.
