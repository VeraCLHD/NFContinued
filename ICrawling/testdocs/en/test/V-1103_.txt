The concern has been that vitamin E supplements increase all-cause mortality—meaning those taking vitamin E live, on average, a shorter life.And now, we know just how much shorter. A QALY, a “quality-adjusted life-year,” is defined as a year of healthy, illness-free life. If you take vitamin E supplements, you live, on average, 0.3 QALYs less than if you did not take vitamin E supplements.
