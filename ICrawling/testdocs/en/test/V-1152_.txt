Anyway, when you hear about environmental estrogens and male fertility, they’re not talking about phytoestrogens—plant estrogens. They’re talking about xenoestrogens—human-made chemicals, like PCBs and pesticides, that can have estrogenic effects. Where are they found the most? Fish.
