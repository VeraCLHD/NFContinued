Most Americans suffer an acute diarrheal illness every year. Certain foods may increase our risk of getting diarrhea such as meat, gluten if we have a gluten sensitivity, and fish. Diarrhea can also be an unwanted side effects for many medications including metformin, an antidiabetes drug, antibiotics, and the diet drug, alli.
